I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Wright State University, Dayton, Ohio. I earned my Ph.D. degree in computer science from the University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign in 1976.

My research interests are Internet Security, Distributed Computing, Software Engineering, and Language Design. I designed a specification and design language called OM. I have directed several MS Thesis and three Ph.D dissertations.
